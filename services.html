Services

What we do?

CONSTRUCTION

JF Foundation is the outcome of the extensive experience of its management team and engineering staff. Our professional approach allows us to provide “Best Value” solutions for our customers. With our wide range of expertise in bored piling works, we have successfully completed projects for a diverse range of clients from both the private and government sectors.

RENOVATIONS

Renovations can often be subtle, improving on the existing building or house. Or, they can be drastic, much like a remodel. Remember construction involving renovation often refers to “restoring” or “repairing” an existing structure, replacing the old with the new.

BIG PROJECTS

Big Projects generally work to change the appearance, structure, or function of a project. In other words, a remodel works to improve upon or transform the existing design and layout of a room. Remodeling refers to “changing” or “transforming” a building or specifics.

CONSULTING

Getting started can sometimes be a daunting task to any homeowner. That’s where our Design/Build expertise can help simplify things. Once we have a basic understanding of what you are trying to achieve, we can design and build a space that perfectly meets your requirements.

ARCHITECTURE

JF Foundation services in several management areas required by any construction project—be it project scheduling, project controls, estimating, constructability reviews, or full-service project management and owner-agent services. Warner’s team of professional consultants helps to identify and minimize project risks for project owners, contractors, and subcontractors alike from the start of design and throughout construction to the project’s final completion and closeout.

DISPUTE RESOLUTION

JF Foundation’s team of expert consultants can provide a significant advantage to a company involved with a project in need of dispute resolution services. Warner evaluates the delay and disruption issues as well as the costs and damages resulting from such issues, then Warner provides the appropriate solution by offering management of claim(s) development, strategic consulting in defense of claims, litigation support services, and expert witness services.
