Director's Message

We have built a successful track record in our business through the emphasis on high quality services. We will continue to strive ahead with our commitment to keep abreast of the continuing demand for better services. By maintaining our commitment of providing quality services and being attentive to the needs of our customers, the company has been continuously successful in securing works from existing and referral customers. The breadth of our experience will enable us to bring forth added value to the projects undertaken, and also to address the needs of our clients, not only from the viewpoint of a contractor, but also from that of the Owner/Consultant. In JF Foundation Pte Ltd, we utilise well-established procedures, modern work methods and practical approach to ensure achievement of quality works within budget and on time. The management of the company is constantly upgrading equipment and skills of staff to sharpen our competitive edge. JF Foundation Pte Ltd is committed to the project deadlines and the pursuit of excellence in terms of customer satisfaction and the quality services. The company adopts a team approach towards project management, providing continuity in construction, fast responding time and enhancing the co-ordination and collaboration of all parties involved in the project. Each member is an integral part of the team and contributes to the successful development to completion of every project undertaken. Our main objectives are: 1. Achieve Safety Standards 2. To Deliver Projects On Time To Our Customers 3. To Limit levels Of Defective Work
