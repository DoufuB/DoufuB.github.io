    Spottiswoode (Singapore)


    Project Brief


    Spottiswoode Residences is a freehold condominium located along 48 Spottiswoode Park Road, 088660, in District 2.

    The project comprises 351 residential units spread across a a 36-storey tower. Spottiswoode Residences was completed in 2014 by UOL Development Pte Ltd.

    This project caters mostly to singles or smaller groups, as units are available in 1 to 3-bedroom layouts. Built--up areas range between 592 to 2,906 sqft

    Including the more luxurious penthouse suites which offer panoramic views of the cityscape, located right at the heart of the city.
